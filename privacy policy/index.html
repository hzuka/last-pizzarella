Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment information when you place an order.
  • Non-Personal Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes such as:

  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Improving our website and customer experience.
  • Sending promotional offers, discounts, and updates (if you've opted in).
  • Responding to your inquiries or feedback.

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and will not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • To payment processors for completing transactions.
  • To delivery partners for fulfilling orders.
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic and performance.

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@pizzasite.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. Please review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, and significant changes will be communicated to you.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
